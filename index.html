🚨 Common Mistakes — Quick Review!

❌ He are a student.✅ He is a student.He/She/It → IS
❌ I have a apple.✅ I have an apple.Vowel sound → AN
❌ There are a cat.✅ There is a cat.One thing → IS
❌ The book is in the table.✅ The book is on the table.Surface → ON
❌ There isn't no milk.✅ There isn't any milk.No double negative!
